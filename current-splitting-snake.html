🐍 Current Snake — Kirchhoff's Junction Law

The snake IS the electric current. It flows through the circuit as one snake, then at the junction it splits into TWO smaller snakes — the split ratio is set by the resistances of the two branches (lower R → bigger snake). At the merge point they combine back. Total in = total out. That's Kirchhoff.
